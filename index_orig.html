Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need the app to use FlossUp?
A: Nope! The physical box works independently — the app is totally optional for tracking and reminders.

Q: Will it work with my favorite floss?
A: Yes! FlossUp is designed to fit standard Oral-B refill containers, but most brands will work with slight adjustment.

Q: How does the pop-up mechanism work?
A: A spring-loaded lift is held down by a timed digital latch. After 24 hours, it releases and pops up with a message.

Q: How long does the battery last?
A: On a single charge, FlossUp lasts about 30 days with regular use.

Q: Can I customize the pop-up messages?
A: Yes! With the app, you can add your own messages or even dedicate each press to someone you love.
